Tales from the Laundromat - Coin Operated gathers the lively voices and stories of launderette owners and their customers across launderettes in the UK. These conversations with artist Kai-Oi Joyce Yung reflect the golddust unseen in and exchanged in the initimate, everyday space of the local launderette.
