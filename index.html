Final Note

Tales from the Laundromat - Coin Operated is a project by artist Kai-Oi Joyce Yung that gathers the lively voices and stories of launderette owners and their customers across launderettes in the UK. These conversations reflect the gold dust unseen and exchanged in the intimate everyday space of the local launderette.

A special thanks to Quiet Down There and Arts Council England for supporting and funding the project, and to Jessie Zhang for developing the site.
